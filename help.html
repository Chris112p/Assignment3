How can we help you?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to our most commonly asked questions:

  • What is your return policy? We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. The item must be in new, unused condition with all tags attached.
  • How do I track my order? You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped. You can track your package on our website or on the carrier's website.
  • What if I need to cancel my order? If your order has not yet shipped, you can cancel it by contacting us. Once your order has shipped, you will need to follow our return policy to receive a refund.
